Pharmacy

Located in the 2nd floor OPD area, Mount Lotus pharmacy provides the full range of drugs & devices used for the treatment of all Eye & ENT conditions. This allows patients to conveniently obtain pharmaceutical products immediately after receiving the prescription from the doctor without having to waste time and money visiting pharmacies outside the premises. We ensure that all our pharmaceutical products are stored according to international storage guidelines in order to maintain the best quality. The qualified pharmacy staff is able to provide up-to-date information which will help you to take your treatments the correct way.
