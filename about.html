About Us

Mount Lotus Hospital is a specialized Eye and ENT hospital which is dedicated to providing a world-class range of Eye and ENT medical solutions to the local and international patients. The hospital is fully equipped with modern medical technologies and highly-acclaimed, skilled medical professionals to provide medical and surgical solutions for any kind of Eye and ENT impairment.

Our highly-qualified, well-trained specialists have years of experience and are backed by a professional, friendly team who are well-versed in taking good care of patients. By using state-of-the-art equipment, they are able to carry out a wide range of Eye and ENT services that will provide you with the best health. Their helpful tips and advice will ensure that you are equipped with the knowledge to take good care of your physical health during your day-to-day lives.
